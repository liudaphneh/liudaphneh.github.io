About

Welcome! I am a survey statistician in the Center for Economic Studies at the U.S. Census Bureau in the EMRI program.

I recently received my PhD in Statistics from the University of Washington, where I was advised by Adrian E. Raftery. My dissertation focused on statistical methods for the analysis and prediction of hierarchical time series data with applications to demography.

Prior to UW, I graduated from Cornell University with a BA in Mathematics.
